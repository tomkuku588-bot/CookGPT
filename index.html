CookGPT 隐私政策

本政策说明 CookGPT 当前 HarmonyOS 版本如何处理与保护用户信息。CookGPT 是离线优先的本地菜谱推荐助手，主要提供菜谱浏览、食材匹配、推荐排序、烹饪步骤、步骤计时、周计划、购物清单、收藏记录和本地备份等辅助功能。

本地优先 收藏、记录、备注、计划和清单默认保存在用户设备本机。
无第三方 SDK 当前版本未接入广告、统计、推送、登录、支付或云服务 SDK。
无敏感权限 当前版本未申请定位、相机、麦克风、通讯录、短信等敏感权限。

一、开发者与联系方式

应用名称：CookGPT。

开发者/个人信息处理者：CookGPT 开发者。

联系邮箱：tomkuku588@gmail.com。如用户对隐私、数据处理或协议内容有疑问，可通过该邮箱联系我们。我们会在收到有效请求后尽快核实并处理。

二、信息处理范围

当前版本默认仅在用户设备本机保存以下数据：

  • 收藏菜谱、做饭记录、备注和未完成的烹饪进度。
  • 周计划、购物清单勾选状态和用户主动导入、导出的备份内容。

我们不会主动收集姓名、手机号、身份证件、通讯录、精确位置、照片、录音、摄像头内容、设备唯一标识、广告标识符或账号信息。应用内食材选择、收藏、记录和备注仅用于本机推荐、记录展示、继续烹饪和数据备份。

三、权限说明

当前版本未在应用清单中申请定位、相机、麦克风、通讯录、日历、电话、短信、通知或后台运行等敏感权限。导入和导出备份仅在用户主动点击对应按钮后调用系统文件选择器，CookGPT 不会扫描用户的其他文件。

四、数据存储、删除与保留

CookGPT 产生的数据默认存储在应用本地沙箱内，保留期限由用户自主控制。用户可在应用内查看、修改收藏和做饭记录，也可以导出备份自行保存。若需删除本地数据，可在系统设置中清除本应用数据或卸载应用；清除数据或重新安装后，应用会重新展示隐私政策与用户协议。

五、第三方共享、转让与公开披露

当前版本不接入广告、统计、推送、登录、支付或云服务 SDK，不向第三方共享、转让或公开披露用户本地使用数据。若后续版本新增涉及个人信息的新功能，我们会在功能触发前提供说明，并在需要时重新取得用户同意。

六、跨境传输

当前版本默认不将用户本地数据上传至服务器，因此不涉及个人信息跨境传输。用户主动导出的备份文件由用户自行保存、移动或分享，请妥善保管。

七、用户权利

用户可以在应用内访问、更正、导出或删除与菜谱、收藏、做饭记录、周计划、购物清单相关的本地数据。用户也可以通过系统设置清除应用数据或卸载应用，以删除本地保存的数据。如需进一步咨询，可通过联系邮箱提出请求。

八、未成年人保护

CookGPT 不以未成年人为主要服务对象。未成年人应在监护人指导和同意下使用本应用，并由监护人确认食材选择、烹饪行为和设备使用安全。

九、安全措施

CookGPT 依托 HarmonyOS 应用沙箱保存本地数据，并尽量减少数据处理范围。用户导出的备份文件离开应用沙箱后由用户自行保管，建议不要将备份文件发送给不可信人员或存放在不安全的位置。

CookGPT Privacy Policy

This policy explains how the current HarmonyOS version of CookGPT processes and protects user information. CookGPT is a local-first recipe recommendation assistant that provides recipe browsing, ingredient matching, recommendation sorting, cooking steps, step timers, weekly plans, shopping lists, favorites, cooking records, and local backup features.

Local-first Favorites, records, notes, plans, and lists are stored on the user's device by default.
No third-party SDKs The current version does not integrate advertising, analytics, push, login, payment, or cloud service SDKs.
No sensitive permissions The current version does not request location, camera, microphone, contacts, SMS, or similar sensitive permissions.

1. Developer and Contact

App name: CookGPT.

Developer / personal information processor: CookGPT developer.

Contact email: tomkuku588@gmail.com. Users may contact us through this email for privacy, data processing, or agreement questions. We will verify and process valid requests as soon as reasonably possible.

2. Scope of Information Processing

The current version stores the following data locally on the user's device by default:

  • Favorite recipes, cooking records, notes, and unfinished cooking progress.
  • Weekly plans, shopping list checked states, and backup content imported or exported by the user.
  • Reading, confirmation, or consent status for the privacy policy and user agreement.

We do not actively collect names, phone numbers, identity documents, contacts, precise location, photos, audio recordings, camera content, unique device identifiers, advertising identifiers, or account information. Ingredient selections, favorites, records, and notes inside the app are used only for local recommendations, record display, continued cooking, and data backup.

3. Permissions

The current version does not request sensitive permissions such as location, camera, microphone, contacts, calendar, phone, SMS, notifications, or background running in the app manifest. Backup import and export call the system file picker only after the user actively taps the corresponding button. CookGPT does not scan the user's other files.

4. Storage, Deletion, and Retention

CookGPT data is stored in the app's local sandbox by default, and the retention period is controlled by the user. Users may view and edit favorites and cooking records in the app, and may export backups for their own storage. To delete local data, users may clear the app data in system settings or uninstall the app. After data is cleared or the app is reinstalled, the app will show the privacy policy and user agreement again.

5. Third-Party Sharing, Transfer, and Disclosure

The current version does not integrate advertising, analytics, push, login, payment, or cloud service SDKs, and does not share, transfer, or publicly disclose local usage data to third parties. If a future version adds new features involving personal information, we will provide notice before the feature is triggered and obtain user consent again where required.

6. Cross-Border Transfer

The current version does not upload local user data to a server by default, so it does not involve cross-border transfer of personal information. Backup files exported by the user are stored, moved, or shared by the user at their own discretion and should be protected carefully.

7. User Rights

Users may access, correct, export, or delete local data related to recipes, favorites, cooking records, weekly plans, and shopping lists in the app. Users may also clear app data in system settings or uninstall the app to delete locally stored data. Further requests may be sent to the contact email.

8. Minors

CookGPT is not primarily intended for minors. Minors should use the app under the guidance and consent of a guardian, and guardians should confirm ingredient choices, cooking behavior, and device safety.

9. Security Measures

CookGPT relies on the HarmonyOS app sandbox to store local data and minimizes the scope of data processing. Exported backup files are controlled by the user after leaving the app sandbox. Users should not send backup files to untrusted persons or keep them in insecure locations.

10. Policy Updates

If future versions add accounts, cloud sync, notifications, advertising, analytics, third-party SDKs, or other capabilities involving personal information, we will update this policy and, where necessary, notify users through in-app dialogs, page notices, or renewed confirmation.
